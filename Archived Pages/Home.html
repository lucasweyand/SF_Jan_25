    welcome to studio fritz

    Craft

    Studio Fritz is dedicated to fine Craftsmanship. We use traditional joinery techniques, only possible through precise hand-tool work, to create complex and unlikely forms. We have never used a screw, and never will. We value a meticulous eye and an intimate understanding of the medium we work.

    Design

    Our design philosophy is rooted in organic form, lasting durability, and practical usability. Each piece begins with an ideation translated to a rough sketch, evolves through numerous CAD iterations, and is brought to life through deliberate material selection and layout.

    Artistry

    Our artistry lies in revealing the beauty within both the perfections and imperfections of each board, resulting in a singular and unique character for every Fritz piece.
