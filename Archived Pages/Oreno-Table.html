    Oreno Coffee Table

    The Oreno Coffee Table is designed to function as both a standard coffee table and a floor-seated tea or dining table. It comfortably seats two people and three if needed. Its use of asymmetry, while maintaining a reference center line, allows for easy and versatile placement within various spaces, blending seamlessly into different surroundings.

    Construction Notes: Floating through tenons reveal the joinery between the base and the hand-shaped table top. The design allows for horizontal running grain in the base. Butterfly keys secure natural checks in the wood.

    Materials: Australian Red Cedar

    Finish: Oil and Wax

    Dimensions: 49"L x 22"W x 12"H

    Design: Studio Fritz, 2023
