    Pangyo Stool

    The Pangyo Stool draws design inspiration from early Japanese and Korean architecture. A subtle curved edge on one side of the seat enhances comfort and provides a distinctive direction to the piece. It is well-suited for use with bar countertops.

    Construction Notes: Wedged mortise and tenon joinery creates a strong connection between the seat and the base. Hand-scooped seat and profiles, hand-turned legs and spindles. Butterfly keys provide stability to the wood checks.

    Materials: Japanese Zelkova

    Finish: Oil and Wax

    Dimensions: 19"L x 9"W x 28"H

    Design: Studio Fritz, 2023
